Our App

Dost is a mobile application that allows users to share their personal health stories and connect with others who have similar experiences.

Our goal is to create a safe and inclusive platform where individuals can connect, share their personal health stories, and offer support to one another. Together, we strive to build a community that promotes healing, empathy, and lasting friendships.

About Us

At Dost, we believe in the power of shared experiences.

Our mission is to create a safe and inclusive platform where individuals can connect, share their personal health stories, and offer support to one another. Together, we strive to build a community that promotes healing, empathy, and lasting friendships.

Our dedicated team at Dost comprises passionate individuals from diverse backgrounds who share a common goal:
to make a positive impact on people's lives. With expertise in technology, healthcare, and community building, we are committed to creating a nurturing environment where connections thrive and healing begins.
